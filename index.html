Agenda of a Milkman

There was, a young lad, hiking up the tip of the nearby mountain of his village. A sour expression masking his face, for he was destined for greatness, but not this. Finally, the tenebrous shaddow of his pray befell him, and he looked up to see, ashen ancient eyes staring him down. His trunk molded into a figure resembling an interrigation mark. How would you like to proceed?

  1. Vent your frustations on the animal.
  2. Find an excuse not to pay.
  3. Pay up.
